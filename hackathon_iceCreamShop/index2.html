About Us


Welcome to Geladinhos Felizes, a small but mighty company that specializes in creating unique and delicious frozen treats.

Our pride and joy is our alcohol ice cream, which combines the creamy goodness of traditional ice cream with the added kick of your favorite spirits.

Our story is simple: we love experimenting with new flavors and techniques to create frozen desserts that are unlike anything you’ve ever tasted. We started selling our alcohol ice cream at local food fairs and quickly gained a following of fans who couldn’t get enough of our boozy creations. Now, we’re excited to share our products with a wider audience through our online store.

At Geladinhos Felizes, we believe that quality ingredients are key to creating outstanding frozen desserts. Whether you’re a whiskey lover or prefer the smoothness of vodka, we have a flavor that will tantalize your taste buds.

We’re proud to be a small business that’s making a big impact on the frozen dessert scene. What we lack in size, we make up for in creativity and passion. We’re always experimenting with new flavors and techniques, so be sure to follow us on social media to stay up-to-date on our latest creations.



Thank you for choosing Geladinhos Felizes. We can’t wait to share our alcohol ice cream and other frozen treats with you!
